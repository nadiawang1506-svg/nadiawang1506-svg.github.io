Work Samples

A sequence of my major and minor work samples.

Major Work - Silk Road Delights

My food technology major work is a Uyghur cuisine food truck menu that is featured at the Lakemba Night Markets annually during Ramadan. I chose this idea to promote and showcase my culture by introducing Uyghur cuisine as an Islamic friendly cuisine originating from China.

Textiles and Design

These images showcase some of the projects I got to create in my first year of university for textiles and design. The pajama set features screen printing, quilting and colour design. The cherry blossom wall hanging features felting, hand embroidery and E-textiles.

Industrial Technology - Wood and Metal

These images showcase projects I created for my minor subject industrial technology (wood and metal). These projects helped me explore out of my comfort zone as someone who has never been inside a workshop, I thoroughly enjoyed creating these projects.
