My Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy as a TAS teacher is to foster creativity, problem-solving skills and critical and creative thinking through hands-on learning and experiences. I believe that empowering students to apply technological knowledge, design thinking and skills will help them develop real life skills assisting them in this ever-evolving digital world. I hope to inspire all my students to reach their creative abilities and showcase their solutions to the world.

“True teachers use themselves as bridges over which they invite their students to cross then, having facilitated their crossing joyfully collapse, encouraging them to create bridges of their own.”
- Nikos Kazantzakis
