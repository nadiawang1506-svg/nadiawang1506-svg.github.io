Interesting Fact About Me

From the age of 4 until 20, I was training for Aerobic Gymnastics. It was my calling, my passion and my love! In 2018, I was selected to represent Australia at the World Championships in Phoenix, Arizona, USA. I placed 2nd in my individual category along with my fellow teammate in 1st. It was the greatest milestone of my sporting career — I would go back in time and do it all over again!
